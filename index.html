About Yuga Spark

A student-run club focused on hackathons, skill-building workshops, and collaborative projects.

Mission

Foster practical problem solving and foster a community where students learn to ship projects fast using design thinking and software engineering best practices.

Vision

Become the region's premiere student hackathon hub that connects talent with real-world mentors and opportunities.

How We Operate

Regular events, mentorship circles, demo nights and an annual flagship hackathon — CodeQuest.
